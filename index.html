My Bio

Clay is a musician living with his family in the Kansas City metro. He has a Bachelor's of Music from Fort Hays State University where he studied percussion with Dean Kranzler, and a Master's of Music from The University of New Mexico where he studied composition and theory with William Wood and Christopher Shultis.
